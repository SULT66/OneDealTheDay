ABOUT US

Less scrolling. Better buying decisions.

OneDailyDrop is an independent deal-discovery site built to surface one standout offer every day and make it easier to understand whether a product is worth your time and money.

Our mission

Online shopping is crowded with endless listings, inflated discounts and repetitive recommendations. OneDailyDrop was created to reduce that noise. We focus on price, customer feedback, product usefulness, availability and retailer reliability.

What makes us different

We do not want to be another giant catalog. Our goal is to explain why a product was selected, show the strongest available buying signals and help shoppers compare offers across trusted retailers.

How we earn money

Some links on OneDailyDrop are affiliate links. We may earn a commission when a visitor makes a qualifying purchase, at no additional cost to the shopper. Affiliate relationships do not guarantee placement or a favorable score.

Who runs OneDailyDrop

OneDailyDrop was founded by Elchin Sultan in Brooklyn, New York, with a focus on building a transparent, data-informed daily deal service.
