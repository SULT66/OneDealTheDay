ABOUT US

A simpler way to check a deal.

OneDailyDrop is an independent deal site. Each day, we choose one main offer and explain why we think it is worth a look.

Our mission

Shopping sites can make every product look like a bargain. We built OneDailyDrop to slow things down and check what matters: the price, customer feedback, usefulness, availability and the seller.

What makes us different

We are not trying to list everything. We choose a smaller number of products, explain why they made the cut and help you compare the offer before you buy.

How we earn money

Some of our links are affiliate links. If you buy through one of them, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. That does not guarantee a product a place on the site or a higher score.

Who runs OneDailyDrop

OneDailyDrop was founded by Elchin Sultan in Brooklyn, New York. The idea is simple: make online deals easier to understand and be honest about how each pick is chosen.
