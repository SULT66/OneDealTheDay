EDITORIAL STANDARDS

Editorial Policy

We want every recommendation to be useful, easy to understand and independent.

Selection standards

We look at the current price, available price history, ratings, review volume, stock, seller reliability and whether the product is actually useful.

Independence

Retailers and brands cannot pay for a positive recommendation or a higher score. If we add sponsored placements, we will label them clearly and keep them separate from our regular picks.

Accuracy

We check the price, availability and main product details before publishing. Retailer information can change quickly, so the checkout page always has the final price and stock status.

Corrections

If we get something important wrong, we will correct it. Send the product name, retailer and page URL to info@onedailydrop.com.

Reviews and ratings

Ratings and review counts come from retailers. We may leave out products when the feedback looks suspicious, too limited or unreliable.

Affiliate compensation

Affiliate commissions help support the site, but the commission rate does not come before shopper value or accuracy.
